# Mercury-Motion — Monetisation Strategy
**Date:** 2026-03-22
**Status:** Draft

---

## The Core Rule

> The free tool makes the premium obvious. Never put capability behind a paywall — only **convenience, scale, and community**.

Color science, film emulation, grain, roto — always free. Paying just means faster setup, managed hosting, or access to the community marketplace.

The MIT core is non-negotiable. It's what builds the community, and the community is what builds the business.

---

## Near-Term (Low Effort, High Value)

| Feature | Why People Pay | Price |
|---|---|---|
| **FilmTools Pro model pack** | Pre-packaged AI models (ESRGAN, SAM2, Depth Anything) — one-click install, auto-updates. Free users install manually. | $20 one-time |
| **Film stock library** | Expanded spectral simulations beyond the free stocks. Community contributes, curated packs sold. | $5–15/pack |
| **LUT marketplace** | Creator-submitted LUTs, revenue share 80/20. Zero cost to build — the community funds itself. | Revenue share |

---

## Mid-Term (Serious Money)

| Feature | Why People Pay | Price |
|---|---|---|
| **Cloud render API** | Self-hostable but managed hosting for teams running batch pipelines. Per-minute pricing. Creatomate charges $0.12/min — we undercut. | Per-minute |
| **Team collaboration** | Shared template libraries, version control integration, CDL round-trip with DIT. Studios pay for this. | Annual subscription |
| **Priority support + SLA** | Agencies and studios pay to have a human respond within hours. | $500–2,000/yr |

---

## Long-Term (If It Blows Up)

| Feature | Why People Pay |
|---|---|
| **Mercury-Motion Studio** | Hosted version — nothing to install, browser only, team accounts. Competes with Frame.io / Creatomate. |
| **Hardware integration** | Partnership with Blackmagic or camera manufacturers. Their cameras already shoot BRAW — we're the only open tool that natively decodes it. |

---

## Why This Works

Remotion got this backwards — they gated the *tool itself* for teams. That's why people look for alternatives. Mercury-Motion's advantage is **trust**, and trust comes from the MIT license being real.

The money follows the community. Build the community first.
